Quick Guides

Maize — Quick Plant

  1. Prepare seedbed; plant at 75 × 25 cm.
  2. Use certified seed; basal fertilizer at planting.
  3. Scout pests weekly and act early.

Tomato — Quick Plant

  1. Nursery then transplant; stake and mulch.
  2. Irrigate regularly; harvest on time.
  3. Use compost + IPM to reduce costs.
