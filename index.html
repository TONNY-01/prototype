Quick Planting Guides

Maize — Quick Plant Guide

  1. Prepare seedbed; plant at 75 × 25 cm.
  2. Use certified seed; apply basal fertilizer at planting.
  3. Scout for pests weekly and take early action.
  4. Top-dress with CAN when plants are knee-high.
  5. Harvest when grains are hard and dry.
🌽 Maize ⏱️ 3-6 months

Tomato — Quick Plant Guide

  1. Start in nursery; transplant after 3-4 weeks.
  2. Stake plants and apply mulch for moisture retention.
  3. Irrigate regularly and fertilize as needed.
  4. Monitor for diseases like blight and pests.
  5. Harvest when fruits are firm and fully colored.
🍅 Tomato ⏱️ 2-3 months
