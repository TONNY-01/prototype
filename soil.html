Kenya planting windows

  • Long rains (Mar–May) — main season for highlands
  • Short rains (Oct–Dec) — window for quick-maturing crops
  • Irrigated lowlands — year-round vegetables with irrigation

Soil advice (general)

  • pH < 5.5: consider liming for many crops
  • Organic matter < 2%: add compost and green manures
  • Sandy soils: increase organic matter to improve water retention

Bio-friendly amendments

Compost, well-rotted manure, rock phosphate and green manures give long-term soil resilience and steady returns.
