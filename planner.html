Manual calculation

Income = yield × price. Profit = Income − (seed + fertilizer + labour). Use these numbers to compare crops and seasons for quicker financial decisions.

Planning tips

  • Use preset templates for common crops to save time
  • Prefer bio-friendly inputs where ROI is similar to reduce long-term costs
  • Factor in time-to-harvest when comparing markets and prices

Example

If yield = 2000 kg and price = 30 KSh/kg, Income = 60,000 KSh. If total costs = 20,000 KSh, Profit = 40,000 KSh.
